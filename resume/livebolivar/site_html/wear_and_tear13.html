Normal wear and tear:

  • Some matting of the carpet.
  • A few small nail holes.
  • Fading or yellowing of the paint. If you've made changes or damaged the paint, you're going to need to repaint.

 

Not normal wear and tear includes but is not limited to:

  • Stains, cuts, and burns on the carpet and the laminate countertops.
  • Broken window glass and cracked ceramic tile.
  • Broken or missing blinds.
  • Gouges and holes in the doors and walls.
  • Pest infestations.
  • Broken cabinet doors, drawers, and closet shelfs.
  • Pet scratch damage anywhere in the unit. This happens mostly frequently around doors, windows, where the animal is confined and includes damage to the floor.
